About Us

Canadian family-owned business, May's Group serves as the dedicated parent company for our three specialized subsidiaries.

Operating from our headquarters in Toronto, we take a hands-on approach to leadership, directly overseeing every aspect of our day-to-day operations to ensure our high standards are never compromised. We are committed to enforcing strict quality control, paramount safety protocols, and responsive customer service across the entire group. By applying disciplined management principles and leveraging modern technology like real-time GPS tracking, we provide a unified and transparent experience for all our partners, from local residents to the many municipalities we serve.
