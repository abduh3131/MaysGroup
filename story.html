Our Story

May’s Group began in 2014 as a small, family-run operation cutting grass and maintaining properties with a simple focus: show up, do the work properly, and treat clients with respect. That consistency led to growth year after year, as customers trusted us with larger responsibilities and higher standards.

Foundation

The founder, Mustafa Tawfiq, studied aviation and is a former pilot. The discipline of aviation, planning, safety, documentation, and precision became the foundation of how May’s Group operates. As demand expanded, we grew from seasonal maintenance into year-round property services, then into larger commercial and municipal work. In 2018, May’s Group was formally incorporated and evolved into a diversified Canadian group with three divisions: Property Maintenance, Construction, and Restoration.

Scale

Today, we service over 40 commercial properties and have successfully completed major projects across Ontario, including over 300 bus stops, parks, and specialized builds such as medical and lab construction.

Standards

What makes May’s Group different is that we invest in people and standards. We consistently train our staff to deliver reliable, safe, high-quality work and we reward long-term commitment.

Hands On

May’s Group remains hands-on and personal. The founder is still heavily involved in day-to-day operations and stays personally in touch with every client, ensuring accountability, quick decisions, and the same high standard on every site season after season.
