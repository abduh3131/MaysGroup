Our Divisions

Three specialized divisions under one roof.

01

Property Maintenance

LANDSCAPE, LAWN & SNOW

Established in 2016, the property maintenance division is the first division of May's Group, offering comprehensive 4-season property maintenance and groundskeeping services. We tailor custom plans to fit your budget while maximizing your property's value. Our services include precise record-keeping with GPS and live camera tracking on all vehicles.

Pictures are taken for every service visit, including walkways, driveways, and lawns, which are instantly uploaded to a cloud organized by date. This allows you to have access to detailed records 24/7, ensuring transparency and accountability. With insurance coverage of up to $5 million, and a 24/7 emergency line, we are your reliable, one-stop solution for property maintenance needs.

02

Construction

OUTDOORS DESIGN & BUILD

The construction division of May's Group, established in 2017, has experienced significant growth, transitioning from residential projects to commercial and municipal undertakings. Our division is proud to be trusted by over 8 municipalities and cities to deliver on major projects.

What sets us apart is our commitment to adding a personal touch to every project we undertake, ensuring that our extensive experience in the field translates into exceptional results.

03

Restoration

INTERIOR DESIGN & BUILD

Our restoration division specializes in meticulous interior renovation and restoration services for commercial and high-rise residential properties. We bring spaces back to life with precision and care.

From emergency repairs to full-scale interior remodeling, our team ensures minimal disruption and maximum quality. We work closely with property managers to deliver on time and on budget.
