Our Divisions

Three specialized divisions under one roof.

Property Maintenance

LANDSCAPE, LAWN & SNOW

Established in 2016, the property maintenance division is the first division of May's Group, offering comprehensive 4-season property maintenance and groundskeeping services. We tailor custom plans to fit your budget while maximizing your property's value. Our services include precise record-keeping with GPS and live camera tracking on all vehicles.

Pictures are taken for every service visit, including walkways, driveways, and lawns, which are instantly uploaded to a cloud organized by date. This allows you to have access to detailed records 24/7, ensuring transparency and accountability. With insurance coverage of up to $5 million, and a 24/7 emergency line, we are your reliable, one-stop solution for property maintenance needs.
